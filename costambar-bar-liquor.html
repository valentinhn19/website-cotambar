Our Story

More Than a Bar —
A Family Tradition

Costambar Bar & Liquor is the newest venture from the family behind Parrillada Costambar Restaurant — Paterson's beloved Dominican restaurant known for authentic cuisine and warm hospitality since day one.

We bring that same passion and community spirit to your neighborhood bar and liquor store. Whether you're grabbing a cold six-pack, a premium bottle, or sitting down for a shot with friends, you're always welcome here.

Hand-selected wines, spirits, beers and cocktails — all in a clean, modern space designed for the Paterson community.
